SpotifyAnalyze

Welcome to SpotifyAnalyze! This site will analyze your recently listened songs on Spotify and suggest new songs to you based on your listening habits. You can then listen to the songs on the site and if you like them you can add them to your Spotify library. Refresh your suggestion playlists to find new songs and keep the good times rolling!

Want More?

If you like the idea of finding new songs based on their emotional pull, you should checkout MoodyTunes! This site allows you to browse songs for an emotion and save them to your personalized playlist. As you add more songs to your emotion playlist the algorithm is better able to suggest songs to you for the emotion, so you can enjoy a more personalized experience for finding new songs.
